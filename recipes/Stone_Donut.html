Sto-nut

It's a rock.

Ingredients

  • Silica
  • Alumina
  • Potassium Oxide
  • Soda
  • Lime
  • Iron
  • Magnesia
  • Titania
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a very hot bowl.
  2. Place now mixed ingredients under immense pressure for millions of years.
  3. Salt to taste.
  4. Let cool, and enjoy!
