Country Sourdough

Look at that tasty cooked dough! Wowza!

How does one cook such a loaf?

Beats me!

But here's my best attempt.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Form the dough into a bread shaped object.
  3. Place bread shaped object in a dutch oven.
  4. Bake at 500 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 minutes.
  5. Let cool, and enjoy!
