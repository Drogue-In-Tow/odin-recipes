Predator

Now THAT'S a tasty looking Alien

Dust yourself off and pull up a chair.

This extraterrestrial hunter is going to

rip the life from your tastebuds.

Ingredients

  • Alien
  • Large cooking pot
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Preheat large pot for 1 hour on high.
  3. Place Alien in pot and cook until golden brown.
  4. Salt with vigor.
  5. Let cool, and enjoy!
